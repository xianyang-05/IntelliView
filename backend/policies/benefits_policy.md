# Employee Benefits Policy v3.0

## 1. Overview

Our comprehensive benefits package is designed to support employees and their families. Benefits are available to all full-time employees from their first day of employment. Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week are eligible for pro-rated benefits.

## 2. Health Insurance

All employees are enrolled in the company's group health insurance plan, which covers:
- Outpatient visits: 100% covered up to $2,000/year
- Hospitalization: 100% covered up to $100,000/year
- Dental: 80% covered up to $1,500/year
- Vision: 80% covered up to $500/year
- Mental health: 100% covered for up to 12 sessions/year with an approved provider

## 3. Adding Dependents

Employees may add eligible dependents to their health insurance and benefits plans. Eligible dependents include:
- Legal spouse or registered domestic partner
- Children under 26 years of age (biological, adopted, or stepchildren)
- Dependent parents (subject to insurer approval)

### 3.1 How to Add a Dependent

To add a dependent, employees must submit the following through the OpenHire self-service portal or by contacting HR:

Step 1: Navigate to "Requests" and select "Add Dependent"
Step 2: Provide the dependent's full legal name, date of birth, and relationship
Step 3: Upload supporting documentation:
  - For spouse: Marriage certificate
  - For children: Birth certificate or adoption papers
  - For domestic partner: Declaration of domestic partnership
Step 4: HR will review and route the request to the benefits provider within 3 business days
Step 5: Coverage for the new dependent typically begins on the first of the following month

### 3.2 Life Events

Dependents may be added outside of the annual enrollment period if triggered by a qualifying life event:
- Marriage or domestic partnership registration
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Loss of coverage from another plan

Life event changes must be submitted within 30 days of the event.

## 4. Retirement Benefits

The company offers a 401(k) plan with a 4% employer match. Employees are automatically enrolled at a 6% contribution rate but may adjust their contribution at any time.

## 5. Wellness Benefits

- Annual wellness stipend: $500 for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Free confidential counseling for personal or work-related issues
- Annual health screening: Fully covered

## 6. Education and Development

- Tuition reimbursement: Up to $5,000/year for degree programs related to the employee's role
- Professional development budget: $1,500/year for conferences, courses, and certifications
- Internal learning platform: Unlimited access to company-curated courses

## 7. Other Benefits

- Commuter benefits: Pre-tax transit and parking deductions
- Employee referral bonus: $5,000 for successful referrals
- Volunteer time off: 2 paid days per year for volunteering
