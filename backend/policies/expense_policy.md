# Expense Reimbursement Policy v2.1

## 1. General Principles

All business-related expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and incurred in the course of performing company duties. Employees are expected to exercise good judgment and spend company funds as if they were their own.

## 2. Eligible Expenses

### 2.1 Travel Expenses
- Economy class airfare for domestic flights
- Business class airfare for international flights over 6 hours
- Hotel accommodation up to $250/night (domestic) or $350/night (international)
- Ground transportation (taxi, ride-share, public transit)
- Mileage reimbursement at $0.67/mile for personal vehicle use

### 2.2 Meals and Entertainment
- Business meals with clients or partners: up to $100 per person
- Team meals: up to $50 per person
- Individual meals during business travel: up to $75/day
- Alcohol is not reimbursable unless part of a client entertainment event

### 2.3 Coworking Spaces
Employees approved for remote work may expense coworking space fees up to $500 per month. This requires prior approval from your manager. The coworking space must be a recognized commercial workspace (e.g., WeWork, Regus, or similar). Home office setup costs are covered under a separate home office stipend and are not part of the expense policy.

### 2.4 Professional Development
- Conference registration fees: up to $2,000 per year
- Online courses and certifications: up to $1,500 per year
- Books and subscriptions: up to $500 per year

### 2.5 Office Supplies and Equipment
- Office supplies for remote workers: up to $200 per year
- Equipment purchases over $500 require pre-approval from IT and your manager

## 3. Non-Eligible Expenses

The following are NOT eligible for reimbursement:
- Personal expenses of any kind
- Fines, penalties, or legal fees
- Gym memberships or personal wellness (covered under benefits)
- First-class airfare (unless medically required with documentation)
- Expenses without valid receipts

## 4. Submission Process

All expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred. Claims must include:
- Original receipt or digital copy
- Business purpose description
- Names of attendees (for meals/entertainment)
- Project or cost center code

Submit all expenses through the OpenHire self-service portal. Claims under $500 require manager approval only. Claims over $500 require both manager and finance approval.

## 5. Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are typically reimbursed within 10 business days via payroll. International expenses are converted at the exchange rate on the date of the transaction.
